## Audit findings (proposed corrections to existing matrix rows)

While preparing this update, the existing `docs/traceability_matrix.md` v0.2 was checked against the locked SDR v1.0 for label accuracy. **Three label findings** were identified — two mislabels and one label tightening. Findings 1 and 3 are corrected by the Section 2 replacement rows above. Finding 2 is corrected by an additional one-row label edit applied as part of the Section 2 approval merge (the row otherwise remains outside Section 2's scope and `pending` until Section 5).

### Finding 1 — Decision 5 mislabeled (corrected by Section 2 replacement)

| | Existing matrix v0.2 label | Correct label per locked SDR v1.0 |
|---|---|---|
| Decision 5 | "Phase 1 baseline models (no-ML factor + calibrated logistic regression)" | **"Benchmark, Sleeve, and Diversifier Treatment"** |

This is corrected by the Section 2 replacement row above. No additional action required.

### Finding 2 — Decision 10 mislabeled (label correction applied with Section 2 approval merge)

| | Existing matrix v0.2 label | Correct label per locked SDR v1.0 |
|---|---|---|
| Decision 10 | "Configuration storage (YAML in git) and rebalance/review cadence" | **"Portfolio Management and Risk Rules"** |

The existing label captures a sub-aspect of Decision 10 (the SDR text does mention "Store thresholds in YAML during early development"), but the Decision is fundamentally about portfolio management — BUY / HOLD / TRIM / SELL / REPLACE / WATCH actions, position counts, equal weight, and review cadence — owned by Section 5.

**Disposition (updated v0.3 per Approver direction):** the label correction is applied as part of the Section 2 approval merge. The Decision 10 row in `docs/traceability_matrix.md` is updated to read "Portfolio Management and Risk Rules" in the *SDR Decision* column. All other cells in the Decision 10 row are unchanged: the row remains `pending` (Section 5 will draft the full replacement row when it owns the implementation). This is a one-cell label edit and does not promote the row's status. Including it now keeps the matrix labels accurate during the gap between Section 2 approval and Section 5 drafting.

### Finding 3 — Decision 4 label tightened (corrected by Section 2 replacement)

| | Existing matrix v0.2 label | Correct label per locked SDR v1.0 |

This is a **label tightening**, not a mislabel: the existing v0.2 label is directionally correct but conflates Decision 4 (ETF launch-date handling) with a Decision 5 concern (benchmark assignments). The Section 2 replacement row above uses the SDR's exact label.
