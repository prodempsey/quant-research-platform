## §2 — Rows extended at v0.8 (Section 5 contributions added; status unchanged)

Extension: Section 5 enforcement of the paper-only Phase 1 boundary at the `portfolio/`, `paper/`, `order_intent/` import surface (no broker SDK imports — test 1) and at the schema layer (`order_intent.order_intents.is_executable = false` CHECK, broker-field NULL constraints — tests 43, 44); no fundamentals, ETF holdings, news/events, earnings transcripts, options data, individual stocks, Danelfin, autonomous research agents, or commercial/customer-facing behavior introduced by `portfolio/`, `paper/`, or `order_intent/`; `backtest.simulated_fills.action` and `portfolio.decisions.action` enums are intentionally disjoint, with CC-09 verified on the Section 5 side via test 33.

- **Decision 3 — Universe survivorship (no historical proxies for delisted ETFs).** Status `in spec`. Extension: Section 5 honors the 03c §12.2 unranked-sleeve disposition for `DiversifierHedge` ETFs whose `rank_method='absolute_with_context'` produces no `models.combined_scores` row under 03c's first-testable formula — Section 5 emits no portfolio action for such ETFs in v1.0 and writes an explanatory issue row per A-PRP-05-16; no silent remapping or substitution; verified by test 32.
